Services

Environmental and Social Impact Assessments

The company is involved in assessment of possible positive or negative impacts that a proposed project may have on the environment including the environmental, social and economic aspects. This is done to ensure that decision makers consider the ensuing environmental impacts when deciding whether or not to proceed with the project.

Environmental Social Management Plans (ESMP)

Preparation of Environmental Social Management Plans is required for formulation, implementation and monitoring of environmental conservation measures during and after commissioning of projects. Management plans are based on resource conservation and pollution abatement considerations among other factors.

Environmental Auditing (EA)

The company does offer the service of environmental audits. These are intended to identify environmental compliance and management system implementation gaps, along with related corrective and preventive actions.
